# Weather (v1)


## Weather Phenomena

fenómeno meteorológico = weather phenomenon `The weather phenomenon was unusual`

rayo (la descarga eléctrica) = lightning `The lightning struck the tree`
relámpago (el destello del rayo)
    = lightning (también se usa aquí) `The lightning was bright`
    = flash of lightning (si se quiere enfatizar) `The flash of lightning was bright`
trueno (el sonido del rayo) = thunder `The thunder was loud`

tormenta (viento, lluvia/nieve y normalmente rayos)
    = storm `The storm was fierce`
    = thunderstorm (enfatiza truenos) `The thunderstorm was fierce`
    = rainstorm (enfatiza lluvia) `The rainstorm was fierce`
    = snowstorm (enfatiza nieve) `The snowstorm was fierce`
    = windstorm (enfatiza viento) `The windstorm was fierce`

nevada = snowfall `The snowfall was heavy`

granizo = hail `The hail was heavy`

niebla = fog `The fog was thick`

inundación = flood `The flood was devastating`

## Types of Weather

buen tiempo
    = good weather `We had good weather on our holiday`

ventoso (con viento)
    = windy `It’s very windy today`
    = breezy `It’s a bit breezy today`

invernal = wintry `It is a wintry thursday`
veraniego = summery `It’s a summery day`
primaveral = springlike `It was a springlike day`
otoñal = autumnal `It was an autumnal day`


## Weather Conditions

cielo despejado = clear sky `The sky is clear today`
cielo nublado = overcast sky `The sky is overcast today`
cielo tormentoso = stormy sky `The sky is stormy today`

calabobos = drizzle `It’s drizzling today`
lluvia ligera = light rain `It’s light rain today`
fuerte lluvia = heavy rain `It’s heavy rain today`
lluvia torrencial = torrential rain `It’s torrential rain today`

chubascos (lluvia intermitente) = showers (ligera o fuerte, pero intermitente) `It’s showery today`
chubascos dispersos/aislados = scattered showers (no son en un sólo lugar) `It’s scattered showers today`

viento fuerte = strong wind `It’s strong wind today`
viento suave = light wind `It’s light wind today`
galerna = gale-force wind `It’s gale-force wind today`

mar tranquilo = calm sea `The sea is calm today`
mar picada = choppy sea `The sea is choppy today`
mar gruesa = rough sea `The sea is rough today`

brisa suave = gentle breeze `It’s a gentle breeze today`
brisa fresca = fresh breeze `It’s a fresh breeze today`
brisa fuerte = stiff breeze `It’s a stiff breeze today`

## Changing Weather

cambiar el tiempo = change in the weather `There is a change in the weather`
se está poniendo ... = is getting ... `It is getting colder`
